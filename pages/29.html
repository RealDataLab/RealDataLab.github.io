The Urban Priority Triage (RenovationMap) is an interactive data visualization platform designed to help municipal authorities, urban planners, and sustainability consultants identify and prioritize buildings for energy efficiency renovations.

By integrating infrared analysis, building age, occupancy, and climate exposure indices, the application maps spatial patterns of thermal inefficiency and highlights areas with the highest potential for energy saving and carbon reduction.

The platform acts as a decision-support tool, optimizing the allocation of public subsidies and private investments to accelerate the transition toward carbon-neutral cities.
